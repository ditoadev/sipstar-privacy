Privacy Policy

💧

Plain and simple: SipStar does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device. No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Ever.

Effective date: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

📱

What SipStar Does

SipStar is a hydration reminder app. It helps you track how many glasses of water you drink each day, sends you friendly reminders through Jal — our water drop mascot — and shows you your weekly progress.

🔒

Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Specifically:

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or crash telemetry
  • No health or biometric data sent anywhere
All hydration logs and preferences are stored only on your device using local storage. They never leave your phone.
🔔

Notifications

SipStar uses local notifications to remind you to drink water. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can turn reminders off at any time from your device settings.

🤝

Third Parties

SipStar does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data brokers. We do not sell, rent, or share any information with anyone — because we do not have any information to share.

👶

Children

SipStar is safe for all ages. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised version here. Since we collect no data, any future changes are likely to be minor clarifications only.
